About Club Portal

Club Portal is a centralized digital platform designed to bring all student clubs, events, and activities under one unified interface.

The portal allows students to explore various clubs, view event galleries, stay informed about upcoming events, and access helpful information through the Q&A section.

This project focuses on clean design, easy navigation, and scalability, ensuring that new features such as event registrations, gallery uploads, and discussion forums can be added in the future.

Built using modern web technologies and the MERN architecture, Club Portal aims to improve student engagement and streamline campus activities through a single, reliable platform.
