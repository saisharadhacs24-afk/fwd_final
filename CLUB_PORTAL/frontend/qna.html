Questions & Answers

How do I join a club? +
Visit the Clubs section, explore available clubs, and contact the respective club committee for membership details.
Who can participate in events? +
All registered students of the college can participate unless specified otherwise in the event details.
Where will event updates be posted? +
Event updates will be available in the Events section and through official club communication channels.
Can I attend multiple club events? +
Yes, students are encouraged to participate in multiple clubs and events based on their interests.
Who do I contact for technical issues? +
For any technical issues, please contact the Club Portal development team or report the issue via the Help section.
